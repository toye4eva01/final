FAQ

Do i need Internet Connection to Perform Transactions?
Yes, All transactions are done online so that you can get realtime feedbacks on your application
Can i Perform Transactions without a card?
No, you must have a valid loyalty card to perform transactions on this application
How can i redeem items for a customer?
You can reedem by selecting the claim benefits from the main menu and filling out the forms
What if i encounter problems using this application?
If problems are encountered please select contact from the main menu and fill the forms therein and click on send when done. PS. make sure you are connected to the internet
How do i perform a Transaction?
You can perform a transaction by selecting the process incentives from the main menu and entering the card details of the customer
